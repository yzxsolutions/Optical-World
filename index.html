FAQ

  • What types of eye tests do you offer?

    We provide both computerized and manual eye testing by qualified optometrists to ensure precise vision assessment tailored to your needs.

  • Do you offer home delivery?

    Yes, we offer free delivery within a 3km radius and courier services to multiple countries with conditional charges.

  • Which brands do you carry?

    We stock over 250 brands, including Essilor, Carl Zeiss, Ray-Ban, Oakley, and more, ensuring a wide selection of trusted options.

  • Are there any free services?

    Yes, we provide free eye testing and spectacle servicing (till stocks last) to ensure your vision needs are met.
